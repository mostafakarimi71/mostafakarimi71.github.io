About Me

I am currently a data and applied scientist in Microsoft. I interned in Ancestry and Anadarko Petroleum as a data scientist in summer of 2019 and 2018. I graduated with a Ph.D. degree at Texas A&M University (TAMU) in Electrical Engineering under the supervision of Professor Yang Shen in 2020. I worked as a graduate research assistant affiliated with the Genomic Signal Processing Laboratory and the Center for Bioinformatics and Genomic Systems Engineering. My research lied at the intersection of Combinatorial Optimization and Machine Learning with applications in Molecular Biology and Genetics. Before joining TAMU, I received a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and a B.S. in Computer Science from Sharif University of Technology (SUT) in 2015. At SUT, I was a member of Advanced Communications Research Institute under the supervision of Professor Farokh Marvasti for three years.
